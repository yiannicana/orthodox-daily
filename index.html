How to use Orthodox Daily

The Calendar tab shows the liturgical calendar for the current day according to the Gregorian calendar (New Style) as observed by many Orthodox churches. Feast titles, commemorations, scripture readings, and the fasting rule are drawn live from the Orthocal API.

Saints whose lives (hagiographies) are available can be expanded by tapping their name. Scripture passages may likewise be expanded to read the full text.
